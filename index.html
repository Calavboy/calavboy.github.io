About Little India

Welcome to Little India, where authentic Indian flavors meet warm hospitality. Our family-owned restaurant has been serving the community for over 15 years, bringing you the true taste of India with traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and authentic spices imported directly from India. Every dish is prepared with love and care, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience that will transport you to the streets of Mumbai, Delhi, and beyond.
